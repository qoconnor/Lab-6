Home tab
  - Shows private posts from friends
  - Option when making a post to be private (Only friends can view)

New tab
  - Shows all new posts that are public
  - Option when making a post to be public (Anyone can see)

Popular Tab
  - Shows must popular public posts

Friendslist Tab
  - Can view friends 
  - Click button next to friend to see their posts

Posts
  - Post a song/artist/album for discussion (Maybe Spotify or Discogs integration)
  - Can choose between public and private
  - Other users can leave comments on a post
  - Users can like a post (Most likes == Most popular)

Private Message
  - Private message other users on your friendlist
  - Inbox/outbox system
